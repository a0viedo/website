About

Alejandro Oviedo is a Backend Engineer based in Barcelona, Spain.

I'm a Senior Backend Engineer with over 10 years of experience in JavaScript, Node.js and Golang. I've worked and collaborated with leading companies in United States, United Kingdom, Argentina and Europe. I have also experience in education, working as instructor for CoderHouse and WebTech Institute (Barcelona).


Outside work I like to brew coffee, organise conferences, listen to music and play the saxophone.
